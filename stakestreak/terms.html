The game

StakeStreak is a social accountability game. Players create or join a "pot" by staking an equal amount of SOL, held in escrow by the service. Once started, every active member must check in within each 24-hour day of the pot. Members who miss a day are eliminated and forfeit their stake. When the pot ends, surviving members split the entire pot equally, minus a 3% service fee.

Fees and purchases

A 3% fee is deducted from the total pot at settlement. A streak freeze may be purchased (0.015 SOL) to retroactively cover a single missed day and restore eliminated status, limited to 2 per pot. Freeze purchases are final and non-refundable.

Refunds

While a pot has not yet been started, any member may withdraw and receive a full refund of their stake via the app. Once a pot starts, stakes are committed and cannot be withdrawn. If a pot is never started, members may claim refunds at any time.

Eligibility and compliance

You must be of legal age in your jurisdiction and legally permitted to use cryptocurrency applications of this nature. Laws regarding games involving staked value vary by country; you are solely responsible for determining whether your participation is lawful where you live.

Risk

Cryptocurrency involves risk. Transactions are irreversible. Only stake what you can afford to lose. The escrow is operated on a custodial basis by the service; while we take security seriously, you accept counterparty risk by participating.
