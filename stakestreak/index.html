StakeStreak

Stake. Survive. Split the pot.

StakeStreak is a social accountability game on Solana Mobile. Stake SOL with friends, check in daily, and survivors split the stakes of those who quit. A 3% fee applies on pot settlement.

Built for the Solana Seeker. Uses Mobile Wallet Adapter and Seed Vault.
