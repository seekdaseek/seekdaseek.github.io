Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

RiskGuard is built by ochinimus for the Solana Seeker. We collect the minimum needed to run the app and nothing more.

What we store

  • Wallet address — your public Solana address, used to identify your one-time alert unlock so it survives reinstalls. We never see or store your private keys or seed phrase.
  • Notification token (FCM) — a device push token, so we can send the price alerts you arm. Used only to deliver those notifications.
  • Alert levels — the price levels you choose to watch (entry, stop, target) and the asset (SOL/BTC), stored so our server can notify you when price is hit.

What stays on your device

Your trade journal and calculator inputs are stored locally on your phone. They are not uploaded to our servers.

What we do NOT collect

No private keys, no seed phrases, no personal identity, no location, no analytics tracking, no selling of data to third parties.

Payments

The 2 USDC alert unlock is an on-chain transaction you sign with your own wallet. We record only that your wallet paid, to restore your unlock.

Deleting your data

Use "Delete account" in the app to permanently remove your unlock record and alert data from our server. Local journal data is cleared with it.
