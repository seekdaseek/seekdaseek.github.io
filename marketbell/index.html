MarketBell
Market hours & macro countdowns, built for crypto traders.

MarketBell shows you how long until every market opens and closes — crypto, New York, London, Tokyo — on one live 24-hour session map. Plus a countdown to the next FOMC decision, the event that actually moves crypto.

Everything is computed on your device. No accounts, no tracking, accurate worldwide, works with no internet.

Free core markets · 2 USDC one-time to add your own (Hong Kong, Frankfurt, Sydney, CME & more). No subscription.

What it does

Session ribbon

See all market sessions overlapping on one 24-hour timeline with a live "now" line.

Time-until countdowns

Opens in 2h. Closes in 40m. The answer a trader actually needs, sorted by what's next.

FOMC macro clock

Countdown to the next Fed decision, with dot-plot meetings flagged high-impact.

Your markets

Add the exchanges you trade. Computed locally, DST-correct, anywhere on earth.

Privacy-first

MarketBell collects nothing. No analytics, no account, no personal data. All calculations happen on your device.
