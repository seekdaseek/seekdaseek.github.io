MarketBell — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MarketBell is built to respect your privacy completely. This policy explains what we do and don't collect.

What we collect

Nothing. MarketBell does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, and no advertising identifiers.

On-device computation

All market times, session calculations, and countdowns are computed locally on your device. The app does not require an internet connection to function.

Wallet and payments

To unlock custom markets, MarketBell uses your Solana wallet via the Mobile Wallet Adapter to send a one-time 2 USDC payment. We never see, store, or have access to your private keys or seed phrase — these remain secured by your wallet and the device's Seed Vault. The only information involved is the public transaction recorded on the Solana blockchain, which is inherently public.

Unlock status

Your unlock status and chosen markets are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded to any server.

Third parties

MarketBell does not share data with third parties, because it does not collect any. Blockchain transactions you make are processed by the Solana network and a public RPC provider; these are public by the nature of blockchain technology.

Children

MarketBell is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page.
